ENTITIES IDENTIFIED
  What: Number of named entities (concepts, policies, people)
  Higher: Better (more comprehensive)
  Typical: RAG 4-8, KG 10-20

LEXICAL DIVERSITY (0-1)
  What: Vocabulary richness
  Higher: Better
  Good: >0.5 | Excellent: >0.7

SEMANTIC COHERENCE (0-1)
  What: Topic connectivity and organization
  Higher: Better
  Good: >0.4 | Excellent: >0.6

INFORMATION DENSITY
  What: Average entities per sentence
  Higher: Better
  Good: >1.0 | Excellent: >2.0

ANSWER QUALITY SCORE (0-100)
  What: Composite metric
  Breakdown: 20% length + 20% diversity + 20% coherence + 
             20% density + 20% entities
  Range: 0-40 Poor, 40-60 Fair, 60-80 Good, 80-100 Excellent
